# Synthesis Template

Use this format when synthesising research or deliberation results.

## Research Synthesis Format

```markdown
## Research Summary: [TOPIC]

### Key Findings

**High Confidence** (all 3 models agree):
- [Finding 1]
- [Finding 2]

**Medium Confidence** (2 models agree):
- [Finding 3]

**Contested/Uncertain**:
- [Finding where models disagree]

### Model Perspectives

| Aspect | gpt | opus | gemini |
|--------|-----|------|--------|
| [Key point] | [view] | [view] | [view] |

### Recommendation

[Synthesised recommendation based on findings]

### Limitations

- [What wasn't covered]
- [Sources of uncertainty]

### Follow-up Questions

- [Suggested deeper research]
```

## Confidence Criteria

**HIGH Confidence:**
- All 3 models agree
- Multiple sources corroborate
- No significant contradictions

**MEDIUM Confidence:**
- 2 models agree
- Some corroboration
- Minor contradictions resolved

**LOW Confidence:**
- Models disagree significantly
- Limited corroboration
- Unresolved contradictions
